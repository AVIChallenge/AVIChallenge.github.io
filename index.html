Welcome to the AVI Challenge 2026!

Assessing True Personality Traits and Cognitive Ability from Asynchronous Video Interviews

Call for Participation: AVI Challenge 2026
The 34th ACM International Conference on Multimedia Grand Challenge invites you to join the AVI Challenge 2026: Assessing True Personality Traits and Cognitive Ability from Asynchronous Video Interviews.

Why Join?
Asynchronous Video Interviews (AVIs) are transforming hiring practices through AI-based assessment of personality traits and cognitive abilities. AVI Challenge 2026 aims to advance methodological rigor and real-world validity.

What Makes This Challenge Unique?
• Realistic & Structured: Interview questions grounded in Trait Activation Theory (TAT).
• High-Quality Annotations: Ratings provided by trained annotators and professional recruiters using BARS.
• Practical Impact: Designed to reflect real-world AVI-based recruitment scenarios.

Be Part of the Future!
Benchmark your models, contribute to fair AI hiring, and join our upcoming multilingual dataset expansion.
